Winter Semester 21/22


Seminar, Algorithm Engineering

The seminar takes place online. We will have a briefing with a discussion of the topics in the first week of the semester, on Monday the 18th October 2021, 4:00pm-4:30pm. The link to participate in the seminar is here. The seminar itself will be a block seminar on the 18th - 19th January 2022. The final reports have to be handed in two weeks after that. Seminar slots will be assigned after the initial briefing. Slides of the initial briefing are here.


More lecture details:


Content: The seminar covers recent topics in Algorithm Engineering. In particular, we will look at recent publications of the best conferences of the field at the moment.

Prerequisites:
Recommended (not necessary):
Algorithmen und Datenstrukturen (IAD),
Algorithms and Data Structures II or Effiziente Algorithmen I (IEA1)

Leistungspunkte: 4 LP

Duration: one semester

Teaching mode: 2 SWS seminar (in English)

Workload: 120h; thereof 30h learning in class, 90h talk and report preparation.

Passing the course: Regular participation in the seminar and discussion. Giving a talk of roughly 45 minutes (including discussion) in English and a written report (also in English) of roughly 7 pages. Giving feedback to other students report via Easychair.

Verwendbarkeit:
B.Sc. Angewandte Informatik,
Lehramt Informatik,
M.Sc. Angewandte Informatik
