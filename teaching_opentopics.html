Open Topics
Most of the time we do have open topics for bachelor and master thesis projects. The topics are designed as real research projects, i.e. the projects are very advanced and require a lot of work, but often result in a publication (depending on the topic, the student and the type of the thesis). If you are interested in writing your thesis with us, send us an email. Below you find example thesis topics, we typically have more topics available.
