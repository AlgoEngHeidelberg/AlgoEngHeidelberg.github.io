Kenneth Langedal
I am a researcher in the algorithm engineering group Heidelberg. In my work, I focus on combining machine learning with conventional algorithmic techniques such as local search or branch and bound. The problems of interest are mainly classical NP-hard optimization problems such as Vertex Cover or Graph Coloring. I am also interested in parameterized algorithms in practice. Especially the use of tree decompositions to solve more (weighted) Vertex Cover instances.
