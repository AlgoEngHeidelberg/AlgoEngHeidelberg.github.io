Marcelo Fonseca Faraj
I am a doctoral student in the algorithm engineering group Heidelberg. My research focus is on graph partitioning and process mapping algorithms. Moreover, I am involved in a DFG project and an FWF project. Currently, I am working on streaming algorithms.
